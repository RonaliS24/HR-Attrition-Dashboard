# HR-Attrition-Analysis-Dashboard

# Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition patterns using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to identify workforce trends and factors contributing to employee turnover, enabling HR teams to make data-driven retention strategies.

# Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to understand why employees leave. Without proper analysis, HR teams cannot identify high-risk employee segments or key drivers of attrition. This project provides a visual analytics solution to monitor attrition and workforce patterns.

# Objectives
- Measure overall employee attrition rate
- Identify departments and job roles with high attrition
- Analyze attrition across salary levels, age groups, and tenure
- Provide insights to support HR decision-making

# Dataset
The dataset contains HR employee information including:
- Employee ID
- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Education Field
- Salary
- Years at Company
- Attrition Status

# Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate
- Attrition analysis by Job Role
- Attrition by Education Field
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition trend by Years at Company
- Department-level filtering

# Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Data Modeling
- DAX Measures
- Data Visualization

# Key Insights
- Most attrition occurs in early tenure employees
- Certain job roles experience higher turnover
- Lower salary slabs show higher attrition rates
- Younger age groups contribute significantly to attrition
